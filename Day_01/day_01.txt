# Day 1 - Introduction to Web Development and SDLC Models

## Topics Covered

1. **Basics of Webpages**
    - Introduction to different types of webpages.
    - Explanation of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
    - Applications and importance of webpages.
    - Reasons for using webpages.

2. **Introduction to MERN and MEAN Stack**
    - Overview of MERN Stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js).
    - Overview of MEAN Stack (MongoDB, Express.js, Angular, Node.js).

3. **Introduction to Node.js**
    - Basics of Node.js and its applications.

4. **Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) Models**
    - Waterfall Model
    - Iterative Model
    - Agile Model
    - Prototype Model

## Summary

On Day 1, we were introduced to the fundamentals of web development, including the different types of webpages and the roles of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. We discussed the applications and importance of webpages and why they are used. We also explored the MERN and MEAN stacks, which are popular frameworks for building web applications.

Additionally, we delved into Node.js, a powerful JavaScript runtime, and its various applications. Finally, we covered different SDLC models, including the Waterfall, Iterative, Agile, and Prototype models, providing a comprehensive understanding of software development processes.
